CSSSA Visual Arts: 5-Day Painting Intensive

California State Summer School for the Arts

August 2–7, 2025 · CalArts Campus

Welcome!

Congratulations! You've been selected to participate in the 2025 CSSSA Visual Arts 5-Day Painting Intensive. This immersive experience takes place at CalArts (Chouinard Hall) and is designed for students demonstrating outstanding promise in painting.

Program Dates

  • August 2–3: Check-in and dorm move-in (Chouinard Hall)
  • August 4–6: Studio Painting Intensive (daily instruction & workshops)
  • August 7: Final showcase & student checkout

What to Expect

  • Daily figure drawing sessions and arts & culture lectures
  • Painting workshops led by professional working artists
  • Field trips to museums, galleries, and studios
  • Studio work on canvas and alternative surfaces
  • Evening open studios and critiques

Packing List

  • Studio-safe clothes (x5 days)
  • Toiletries, towels, Twin XL bedding
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Optional sketchbook/tools
  • Water bottle, sunscreen, chargers

Dorm Info

You’ll stay in Chouinard Hall, located on the CalArts campus. Rooms are double-occupancy with shared bathrooms. Bedding and towels not provided. Resident Assistants and CSSSA staff provide 24/7 supervision.

Program FAQs

  • Is this program free? Yes — tuition and housing are fully covered.
  • Are meals included? No, but campus dining and grocery options are available.
  • Who can attend? Students entering grades 9–12 or graduating high school in 2025.
  • Should I bring art supplies? Most supplies are provided, but personal sketchbooks/tools are welcome.
